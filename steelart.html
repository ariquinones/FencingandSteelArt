Steel Art in Houston

As a professional welder, you could imagine Bardo enjoys welding in his spare time. We receive various steel art requests from friends and clients. If it involves steel art, welding, imagination and creativity, chances are, Bardo can do it. It's worth stating that we won't take on a steel art project we know we won't be able to perfect. However, Bardo welcomes any challenge and clients are always satisfied with the end product. Some of our projects consist of steel tables, a giant tree, a life size giraffe, AC unit cages, company logos, and minions (yes, we said minions). Bardo genuinely enjoys bringing steel ideas to life.
